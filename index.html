About the Avatar Universe

Pandora

Pandora is a distant moon rich in life and energy. Its ecosystem is deeply connected, and every living being plays a role in balance.

The Story

The story follows the Na’vi people and their struggle to protect their world. It is a journey about identity, choice and connection.

Explore the lands, creatures, and stories of a living planet

Jake Sully enters Pandora through the Avatar Program and discovers the Na’vi way of life. What begins as a mission turns into a choice between loyalty and belonging.
Jake and Neytiri build a family and are forced to leave their home to protect it. As they explore Pandora’s oceans, they learn new ways of living and fighting together.
A new chapter reveals darker sides of Pandora and the Na’vi. The balance is challenged not only by humans, but from within the world itself.
